Brain Music — Support

Brain Music is an ambient listening app composed by Hiroaki Yokoyama, with four modes: Focus, Sleep, Nap, and Lullaby.

Contact

Questions, feedback, or issues can be sent to yuricewine@gmail.com. We read every message and typically reply within a few days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use the app?
No. Brain Music works fully offline with no sign-in required.

Is my listening data collected or shared?
No. Brain Music does not collect any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The audio isn't playing / a mode isn't working.
Please email yuricewine@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version, and we'll help you resolve it.

よくある質問

アカウント登録は必要ですか？
不要です。Brain Musicはオフラインで動作し、ログインは必要ありません。

再生データは収集・共有されますか？
一切収集していません。詳細はプライバシーポリシーをご覧ください。

音声が再生されない／モードが動作しない場合は？
yuricewine@gmail.com まで、お使いの端末機種とiOSバージョンを添えてご連絡ください。
